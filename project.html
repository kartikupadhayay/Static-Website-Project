Who We Are

Powering India's Industrial Growth

Shriji Optimum Solution Pvt Ltd is a dynamic Electro-Mechanical company established in 2024, headquartered in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh. We bring together advanced technology, skilled manpower, and customer-focused strategies to deliver cost-effective and sustainable engineering solutions.

Shriji Optimum Solution have also tied up with various Expert Agencies for carrying out specialized jobs like Radiography, Heat Treatment, and Thickness Measurement and to provide Experts (Ex.BHEL) in the Power Sector.

We specialize in Turnkey Projects, Annual Maintenance Contracts, Overhauling, and complete project execution for Solar and Thermal Power Plants (BTG, CHP, BOP) — maintaining an unwavering commitment to innovation, safety, and technical excellence. We hold an A-Class Electrical Licence of Uttar Pradesh.
